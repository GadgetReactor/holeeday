About Holeeday.

Aren't public holidays just awesome? It's extra special if it falls on a Friday or Monday for the super long weekend. Perfect for planning a quick getaway. Holeeday is a tool to help you cope while waiting for the next holiday. It automatically countdown to the next public holiday based on your country location.
